In the Darkness

‘In the Darkness’ is an interactive storybook app for children aged 6-8, the aim of which is to encourage dialogue between children and parents, and to help children to understand and rationalise their fear of the dark.


‘In the Darkness’ is about a boy called Freddie. He has just moved into his grandmother’s old house and he hates it. The house has been empty for a few years and has fallen into disrepair. Freddie’s parents plan on renovating it but have yet to start. The house is surrounded by a dense, eerie forest. It’s the spookiest house Freddie has ever seen. The state of the house feeds into his fear that ghouls and monsters may lurk in it. When Freddie goes to bed at night, a shadowy creature appears at the edge of his bed. This creature is the Fear. It scares him by telling him about all the scary things hiding in his room. Freddie decides to hide out downstairs, away from the monsters. He doesn’t realise that every room in the house has strange creatures lurking in it. As Freddie makes his way around the house, he has to face different monsters, each a little scarier than the last. He gradually gets a little bit braver, until he is finally brave enough to tackle the monster under the bed. But these creatures are a product of Freddie’s imagination. When the reader taps on each one, the monster’s true identity is revealed.
